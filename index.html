    • Research Experiences

    Huawei Ascend Chip Development - (Project Manager)
    • I led a team to design and develop an intelligent voice note-taking platform for teaching scenarios based on Huawei’s Ascend 310 chip, in which emotion and speech recognition algorithms are migrated to the Atlas 200DK terminal, achieving real-time recognition of academic emotions of students and lecture speech of teachers. This platform has been applied to the actual teaching process at Jilin University and has been included in the Huawei Ascend Developer Community.
    • Based on Huawei’s Ascend 910 chip, we migrated advanced deep learning algorithm such as BCE from traditional GPUs to Huawei NPU chips, achieving full-stack migration tasks from model training to accuracy tuning.
    • During the migration process, we often encountered situations where the underlying operators were not supported, such as the next operator being incompatible during data iteration. We helped Huawei personnel adjust and develop chip underlying operators.
    National Natural Science Foundation of China - (Project Manager)
    • Led the team to design and develop academic emotion recognition algorithms for real teaching scenarios.
    • Led the team to develop a teacher teaching behavior recognition algorithm that integrates psychological domain knowledge.
    • led the team to study long-tail recognition and optimization algorithms at the feature level. Related papers were published in CVPR 2023.
    Government Affairs Map Construction Project - (Algorithm Researcher)
    • For more than 600,000 policy text data in Jilin Province, automatic classification is performed from multiple dimensions such as subject and genre. The words in the policy text data are vectorized, and the LSTM model is used to extract text features to achieve multi-label classification. The classification accuracy reaches 98%, and a policy knowledge map is constructed based on labeled policy documents to support policy query applications.
    • Based on government legal documents, a matter map is constructed. Based on Bert, LSTM network and CRF module, entity recognition is performed on labeled legal documents, and the recognition accuracy can reach more than 80%.
    • Based on the entities such as matters, laws, and departments in the policy documents, a government affairs map is constructed, using the Neo4j graph database to store entities and relationships, and supporting upper-level government intelligent question answering applications.
    European Union Horizon 2020 Project - (Algorithm Researcher)
    • Participated in the development of the mobile sensing platform architecture, responsible for the development of the collection and preprocessing module for multimodal sensor time series data, and applied to social behavior data collection and analysis experiments in 13 universities in 10 countries. Led the team to develop a localized mobile sensing platform iSense for Chinese student social behavior analysis, and conducted two data analysis experiments with more than 50 people at Jilin University.
